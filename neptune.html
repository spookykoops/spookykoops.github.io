Is it possible to live on Neptune?

Life on Neptune is not possible. This is because the planet contains no oxygen, which is an essential factor for life. The temperatures on Neptune are also extremely lethal.


How far away is earth from Neptune?

Earth is approximately 2.7 billion miles apart. Using space travel, it is possible to reach Neptune in about 12 years.

All about Neptune and its surface

Neptune is one of the eight planets in our solar system. It's climate is very active, causing many storms. Neptune also has 14 moons that orbit it. The temperature can often hit negative 201 degrees celsius. There has also been one spacecraft that has ever passed by Neptune, which was the Voyager 2.


Who is Poseidon?

Neptune was a planet named after the god of the sea. This roman god's name was Poseidon. The planet and the god are related because of the similar color Neptune has to the sea.
